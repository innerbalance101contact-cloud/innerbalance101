The Equilibrium Mark — Usage Rules

Circle with horizontal balance line. Two clay dots. Dashed vertical axis. Never stretch, recolour, rotate, or add effects. The mark represents internal equilibrium — stable, measured, and in balance.

04

Brand Voice

✓
The brand says
  • You are not broken. You are overloaded.
  • Come back to yourself.
  • Not a remedy. A discipline.
  • Mental strength is trained, not gifted.
  • Recognition before solution.
  • The system builds the foundation. The library sustains it.
✕
The brand never says
  • Trust the universe.
  • Healing journey.
  • Manifest your best self.
  • High vibe only.
  • Feel the magic.
  • You deserve to thrive.
01

No exclamation marks

Anywhere. In any copy. The brand is grounded, not excitable. Conviction needs no punctuation to perform.

02

Short sentences

Declarative. Direct. One idea per sentence. The reader is already carrying a lot. Make it easy to receive.

03

No em dashes in body

Resolve with a full stop or new sentence. Em dashes interrupt the calm register the brand holds.

04

Lead with recognition

Open with what the reader feels before offering the solution. They need to feel seen before they can receive anything.

05

Specific transformations

Overwhelm → Grounded. Pressure → Self-assurance. Sell the destination, not the emotion. Never "feel better."

06

Two voices, never mixed

Brand voice (80%): smart, structured, science-forward. Founder's Mirror (20%): raw, soul-level, personal. Keep them distinct.
