You can name your goals.
What's harder to name is
how you actually feel.

Most people can tell you what's on their plate. Very few can tell you what's running underneath it. The pressure that's there even on good days. The depletion that sleep doesn't fix. The noise you've learned to work around.

This assessment helps you see it clearly. It starts with your body, then looks at how well you notice what's happening inside, then maps which emotions are carrying the most weight right now.

Five minutes. No right answers. Just slow down enough to be honest.
What you'll leave with
  • A clear picture of what you're actually carrying
  • Which emotions are running loudest right now
  • Where to start
