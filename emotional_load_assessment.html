Introduction
The Emotional Load Assessment

Before you can regulate,
you need to know
what you're carrying.

You can probably name your goals, your KPIs, your next move. What's harder to name, with any real precision, is what's actually happening inside you while you do it all.

This one works differently. It doesn't start with a questionnaire. It starts with your body. Then it looks at how connected you are to your inner experience. Then it maps your emotional load.

This takes around 8 minutes. There are no right answers. The most useful thing you can do is slow down enough to answer honestly.
What you'll leave with
  • A clear read of your current emotional load level
  • An honest look at your self-awareness and somatic connection
  • A personalised result profile, not a generic score
  • One specific practice recommended for where you actually are

Phase 1 of 3: Body Check-In

Right now, where do you feel tension in your body?

Before you answer anything with your mind, pause for a moment. Take a breath. Then notice: not what you're thinking, but what you're actually feeling in your body. There's no right answer here. Even not knowing is data.
