Introduction
The Emotional Load Assessment

Before you can regulate,
you need to know
what you're carrying.

Most high-achieving professionals can name their goals, their KPIs, their next move. What they struggle to name — with any precision — is what's happening inside them while they do it all.

This assessment is different. It doesn't start with a questionnaire. It starts with your body. Then it measures how connected you are to your inner experience. Then it maps your emotional load.

This takes around 8 minutes. There are no right answers. The most useful thing you can do is slow down enough to answer honestly.
What you'll leave with
  • A clear read of your current emotional load level
  • An honest look at your self-awareness and somatic connection
  • A personalised result profile — not a generic score
  • One specific practice recommended for where you actually are
Phase 1 of 3 — Body Check-In

Right now, where do you feel tension in your body?

Before you answer anything with your mind, pause. Take a breath. Then notice — not what you're thinking, but what you're physically sensing. There's no right answer. Even not knowing is useful information.
